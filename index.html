Farming

We grow a large variety of crops at Sunshine Farm. We specialize in growing coffee plants, green tea bushes, and ancient fruit. Melons, tomatoes, potatoes, and green beans are also plentiful. As far as fruit trees go, we have cherry, apple, and apricot.

Animals

This section will be all about the aminals on the farm. We have cows, chickens, goats, ducks, a pig, a dinosaur, and a kitty cat on the farm.

Artisan Goods enhabit a very special place in our hearts here at Sunshine Farm. Duck mayo, cherry jelly, green tea, and truffle oil are some of our prized Artisan Goods!
